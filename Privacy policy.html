PRIVACY POLICY

At Design SolTech, we aim to protect the security and privacy of our valued customers regarding personal information.
Our system collects your personal information when you fill out any form on our website, either as a client or an applicant.
We might also use your information, for example, email and phone number, to send you promotional messages for marketing purposes.
Design SolTech ONLY discloses your personal information to our trusted authorities, that is, our designers, developers, and financers, if needed.
We take appropriate measures to secure your personal information from disclosure and unauthorized access.
We also use cookies to improve your browsing experience. However you can select to disable cookies, but this might limit your access to certain features of Design SolTech's website.
Our website might contain third-party websites links that we cannot control. These links might use your Limited Personal Information, so we are not accountable.
We might update this Privacy Policy by posting a new version of the privacy policy on our website. We highly encourage you to analyze this policy to stay tuned with our data- gathering practices.
If you have any concerns or questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@designsoltech.com.
